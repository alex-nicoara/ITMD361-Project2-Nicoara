Autobiography

Early Years

I, Alexander Mitchell Nicoara, was born on April 8th, 1995 in Park Ridge, IL on a cold, rainy day. I lived in Elk Grove Village, IL with my parents, and was an only child until my brother entered the world 2 years later. From what I know from my parents, I had a lot of energy as a young child and liked to draw.

Childhood

As a gradeschool kid, I was ambitious and always hanging out around the neighborhood with friends.

College Years

My college years included some of the best and worst moments of my life. School became difficult for me and pushed me to be at my best every day. While I had success early on, I experienced a bit of a slump in my grades late sophomore year. I realized that I had to make sacrifices so I could perform well in school, and so I made some to increase the amount of time I could spend on my schoolwork. This paid off, and I made the Dean's list with a near perfect GPA the following semester. While in college, I was also involved in some organizations and a sport. I was a distance runner for track and field for a semester, an active member of the Phi Kappa Sigma Fraternity, and involved in Cru (a Christian campus ministry). Being involved really helped me grow and mature personally and helped me overcome social anxiety.

Future

As I look into the future, I hope to get a job in Web Development. Web Development is something I really enjoy doing and learning about. I also want to travel and experience different parts of the world

Autobiography

Early Years

I, Alexander Mitchell Nicoara, was born on April 8th, 1995 in Park Ridge, IL on a cold, rainy day. I grew up in Elk Grove Village, IL with my parents, and was an only child until my brother entered the world 2 years later. From what I know from my parents, I had a lot of energy as a young child and was adventurous.

Pre-Teen/Teen Years

Growing up as a pre-teen/teen was fun to say the least. I was given my first computer in 6th grade. I remember playing so many games on sites such as miniclip, addictinggames, and newgrounds. It really sparked my first interest in learning how to program, and from there it took off and became something I thought of doing as a career. During this time of my life I was involved in boy scouts, soccer, and karate. I also played chess, and placed in the top half of the rankings at the state tournament.

College Years

My college years included some of the best and worst moments of my life. School became difficult for me and pushed me to be at my best every day. While I had success early on, I experienced a bit of a slump in my grades late sophomore year. I realized that I had to make sacrifices so I could perform well in school, and so I made some to increase the amount of time I could spend on my schoolwork. This paid off, and I made the Dean's list with a near perfect GPA the following semester. While in college, I was also involved in some organizations and a sport. I was a distance runner for track and field for a semester, an active member of the Phi Kappa Sigma Fraternity, and involved in Cru (a Christian campus ministry). Being involved really helped me grow and mature personally, also helped me overcome social anxiety.

Future

As I look into the future, I hope to get a job in Web Development. Web Development is something I really enjoy doing and learning about. I also want to travel and experience different parts of the world.
